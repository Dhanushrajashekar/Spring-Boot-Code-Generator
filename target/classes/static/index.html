What is Pagination?
Imagine your table has 50,000 records. Loading all of them at once would be slow and wasteful — like asking a library to bring you every book at the same time just because you want one. Pagination lets you load a small chunk at a time, like "give me records 1–20, then 21–40" and so on. Your API will gain a GET /page?page=0&size=20&sort=id,asc endpoint that the frontend can call to fetch one page at a time.
